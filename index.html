About Us

Nurturing the Future of Mayurbhanj

Medhavi Mayurbhanj is a flagship education initiative under the Department of Education, Mayurbhanj, Odisha. Our mission is to bridge the education gap in tribal and rural areas by providing quality learning resources, teacher training, and digital literacy programs.

We believe every child in Mayurbhanj deserves access to world-class education. Through our comprehensive programs spanning across Rairangpur, Karanjia, and Udala blocks, we are transforming classrooms and empowering the next generation of leaders.
